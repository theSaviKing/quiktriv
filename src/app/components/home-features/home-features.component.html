What can I do with QuikTriv?

Create
Make your own trivia games and quizzes with ease! Write your own questions or import them from the Open Trivia Database.
Play
Play any of the thousands of trivia games and quizzes created by our community! Search for quizzes to play right now or save them to your account to play later.
Track
Create an account to track your progress and see how you stack up against other players! Earn achievements and badges for completing quizzes.
Share
Send your quizzes to friends and family to see how they do! Generate a link to any quiz you make and share it for anyone to log in and play.
